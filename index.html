AboutMe

Hello! I'm Onella, a vibrant and enthusiastic individual originally from the beautiful island of Jamaica. For the past 13 years, I've been calling Atlanta my home, and it's here that my passion for technology and creativity has truly blossomed. My journey into the world of computers started back in high school, where I took IT courses and discovered a deep fondness for both computers and design.

In January of 2020, I decided to dive deeper into this passion by taking an introductory coding course with SheCodes.io. It was a transformative experience that revealed my love for coding and set me on the exciting path of becoming a Front-end Web Developer. Since then, I've been eagerly honing my skills, constantly learning, and enthusiastically embracing every new challenge that comes my way. I'm driven by a genuine curiosity and a relentless desire to create beautiful, functional websites that make a difference. Let's code something amazing together!
